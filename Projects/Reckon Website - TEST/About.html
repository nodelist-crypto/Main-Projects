OUR CORE VALUES

Since our inception in 2010, as an engineering based company in the field of Telecommunication and construction, we have operated under the guidance of these core values:

  • We succeed through satisfied customers
  • We deliver quality and excellence in all we do
  • We use technology to develop market leadership
  • We value our employees
  • We behave responsibly as a corporate citizen

VISION

To be the leading innovator in Telecom and Civil services, driving progress through cutting-edge solutions and making a positive impact on the industries and communities we serve.

MISSION

At RECKON Engineering System, our mission is to provide outstanding Telecom, Civil, and General Order Supply services. We are committed to innovation, continuously improving our systems, and exceeding our customers' expectations. We value our employees and are guided by our core values of Radiance, Heart, and Belonging. By adhering to high ethical standards, we strive to have a positive impact on our industry and the communities we serve, building a company that we can all be proud of.

CEO’s Message

Our responsibilities extend beyond the horizons of offering the Telecom, Civil and General order supply services. Adaptation of the new technologies and enhancement and improvement of the systems has always remained a continual process for the organization.

At RECKON, we enjoy this opportunity, as it brings out the best in us, striving to provide our valued customers with our greatest efforts at all times. We are motivated to remain an innovative market leader, surpassing our customers’ expectations, and inspiring the communities in which we operate. At RECKON, we pride ourselves on our foundations, on our employees and in the way we operate, which is in line with the objectives of our corporate strategy that revolves around our core values. These values are: Radiance, Heart and Belonging.

RECKON has played a pivotal role over the years in helping to facilitate all related telecom services; (Civil, BSS tower fabrication and Commercial Power Energization, Acquisition etc.), which is a source of pride for us. We know that today’s world is complex and we will continue to face situations that do not have obvious solutions. In the face of this complexity, the ethical guidelines of the Code of Conduct exist to guide us and to empower us to make the right choices as individuals and as a company. Adhering to the Code of Conduct is the responsibility of each and every one of us in RECKON. By doing this we can have a positive impact in our industry, communities in which we operate, and build a company we can be proud of, where corporate responsibility is a key competitive advantage.

We’ll look forward to evolve with you,

CEO – RECKON Engineering System
