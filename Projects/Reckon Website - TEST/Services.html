RECKON Engineering System delivers comprehensive power solutions, focusing on both conventional and renewable energy sources to meet the diverse needs of our clients.

1. Telecommunication Services

Overview: RECKON Engineering System offers a wide range of telecommunication services, ensuring reliable connectivity and advanced communication solutions for clients across various sectors.

Key Services:

Site Acquisition: We assist clients in identifying and acquiring suitable sites for telecommunication infrastructure, ensuring compliance with local regulations and zoning laws.

Installation and Commissioning: Our team specializes in the installation and commissioning of Base Transceiver Stations (BTS), RF equipment, and microwave systems, ensuring seamless integration and optimal performance.

Network Optimization: We provide services for network optimization, including drive testing, performance monitoring, and troubleshooting to enhance network efficiency and reliability.

O&M Support: Our Operations and Maintenance (O&M) services ensure that telecommunication systems operate smoothly, with regular maintenance and prompt response to any issues.

2. Solar Energy Solutions

Overview: RECKON is committed to promoting sustainable energy solutions through comprehensive solar energy services that cater to both residential and commercial clients.

Key Services:

Solar Panel Installation: We provide end-to-end solar panel installation services, ensuring optimal placement and integration with existing systems to maximize energy efficiency.

System Design and Engineering: Our team designs customized solar energy systems tailored to the specific energy needs of our clients, considering factors such as location, energy consumption, and budget.

Maintenance and Support: We offer ongoing maintenance and support services to ensure the longevity and efficiency of solar installations, including regular inspections and performance monitoring.

Consultation Services: We provide expert consultation to help clients understand the benefits of solar energy, including cost savings, environmental impact, and available incentives.

3. Power

Overview: RECKON Engineering System delivers comprehensive power solutions, focusing on both conventional and renewable energy sources to meet the diverse needs of our clients.

Key Services:

Power Generation Solutions:  We design and implement power generation systems, including diesel generators and renewable energy solutions, to provide reliable power supply for various applications.

Electrical Installation:  Our team handles the installation of electrical systems, including power distribution boards, motor control centers, and generator control panels, ensuring compliance with safety standards.

Energy Management: We offer energy management services to help clients optimize their energy consumption, reduce costs, and improve overall efficiency.

Consultation and Design:    Our experts provide consultation and design services for power systems, ensuring that clients receive tailored solutions that meet their specific energy needs.

4. Fabrication & Galvanization

Overview: RECKON Engineering System specializes in mechanical fabrication and galvanization services, providing high-quality solutions for various industrial needs.

Key Services:

Mechanical Fabrication: We design and manufacture custom-built structures, including industrial sheds, steel trusses, and solar structures. Our fabrication process ensures durability and compliance with industry standards.

Hot Dip Galvanization: Our galvanization services protect metal structures from corrosion, extending their lifespan. We utilize advanced techniques to ensure a uniform coating that meets international quality standards.

Project Management:   We oversee the entire fabrication process, from design to installation, ensuring timely delivery and adherence to project specifications.

Quality Assurance:  Our team conducts rigorous quality checks at every stage of the fabrication process to ensure that all products meet the highest standards of quality and safety.
